TattooReserve is a modern SaaS platform purpose-built for tattoo artists and studios to manage
the full client lifecycle — from discovery to booking to payment.

Clients can browse artist profiles, explore portfolios and flash designs, and request
appointments through a booking flow designed for the realities of tattoo work. Artists get a
dashboard to manage reservations, showcase their portfolio, publish flash with limited-edition
runs, and accept payments and deposits through Square and Stripe.

Scheduling is timezone-aware end to end, so an artist in one city and a client in another always
see appointment times correctly, including across daylight-saving changes. Google Calendar
integration keeps an artist's existing calendar in sync, and searchable, location-aware profiles
make artists easy to discover. Throughout, anti-spam filtering and file-safety screening protect
artists from junk requests and unsafe uploads.
